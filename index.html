Literature Survey

Healthy people are an asset to a country. It is clearthat it is one of the most valuable resources in the country compared to other resources. The health of the country’s labor resources is a key factor in strengthening the work world. Energetic, youthful energy depends on good health conditions. Therefore, it is very important for people to be healthy in order to maintain their capacity. The prevalence of heart and lung diseases is increasing along with global population growth. In the future, a system for early human health diagnostics will be required.

The purpose of this study is to enable an individual to get an idea of his or her health status by retrieving data from a sensor device without having to go to the hospital and to provide an active packaging function through web development. Additionally, an X-ray examination of the patient is used to make the diagnosis and predict the disease’s current state. Once the ailment has been identified using these two techniques, a prescription is created for the identified condition and the closest pharmacies and hospitals.




Research Gap

There have been accounts of persons suffering from numerous maladies as a result of their usage of tobacco, alcohol, and an improper diet. According to the knowledge we can glean from people suffering from such disorders, these are the gaps we had identified in this research.

  • Identify some current situation measurements of a person by using a device.

  • Identify heart related diseases according to the risk level.

  • Identify the lungs operation by using X-ray of a patients.

  • Provide a future diseases prediction to the people that describes future upcomings.


Research Problem

  • There are some diseases that have arisen at present. (Heart attack, Pneumonia, Wheezing, Dengue, Covid’19)

  • High cost for diagnosis.

  • Informal lifestyle and busyness of people.

  • People don’t have enough idea about current situation of the country.


Objectives

Main Objective

The primary goal of this research is to provide patients with the essential remedies and requirements for their existing ailments, as well as to discover chronic disorders in their bodies. And also provide a current situation diseases activation of the country as an prediction to the people.

Specific Objective

The specific aims are as follows, according to the preceding major objective explanation:

  • Expert gadget creation to gather and analyze sensor data.
  • Disease level-wise analysis and provide recommendations.
  • Heart and lung diseases identify using image processing techniques.
  • Provides the current situation diseases information in the country.

Methodology

Device implementation

This study includes a device that has been implemented. This will involve getting current patient body parameters and sending those to the firebase to analyze the disease.


Heart disease level-wise identification

By gathering user information, this component will analyze heart-related diseases (heart attack, pneumonia, wheezing).Then, provide the current risk level as well as recommended next steps.



Future diseases forcast

There should be a way to know future disease activation analyses, and by referring to them, people should be able to prevent such diseases. Therefore, this will provide a future disease prediction (Dengue, Pneumonia, Heart attack).


Use image processing techniques

X-ray images are used in this case to identify the patient's disease (Covid, pneumonia, lung cancer) and lung opacity using image processing techniques.
